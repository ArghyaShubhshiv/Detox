About Detox

Detox is like a godfather for your digital life, it reminds you to take little happy breaks every hour, so that you can spend your life outside of your screen.
It customizes the break time according to your age and enables the reminders in such a way that you can do work on your screens and spend time with your loved ones.
We're here to Reduce Screen Fatigue, one break at a time...
